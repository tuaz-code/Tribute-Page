Dr. Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala

The woman who redefined the Nigeria Economy

Okonjo-Iweala had a 25-year career at the World Bank in Washington DC as a development economist

Here's a time line of Okonjo-Iweala:

  • 1954

    Birth

    Born in Ogwashi-Ukwu, Delta State, Nigeria

  • 2007-2011

    Career

    Previously, Okonjo-Iweala spent a 25-year career at the World Bank as a development economist, scaling the ranks to the Number 2 position of Managing Director, Operations

"The U.K. and the U.S. could not have been built today without Africa's aid. It is all the resources that were taken from Africa, including human, that built these countries today! So when they try to give back, we shouldn't be on the defensive."

-- Dr. Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala
